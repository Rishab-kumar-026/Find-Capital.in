FAQs

Why to Choose FindCapital?

FindCapital is a go-to platform for any startup who are looking for funds, and connects then to their potential investors. Unlike our competitors, we ensure the smooth conduction of the investment process which is simple and legal..

What procedure does FindCapital follow?

FindCapital lists the investors according to the Startups niche, making conversation happen between both the parties, Generating the legal documentation procedure, and finally, startups accessing the funds..

Is the Data Legal?

Yes. The data displayed of both the parties (investors and startups) are taken with consent and permission of both the parties.

Are the Investors Genuine?

Yes, Absolutely. The investors displayed on FindCapital are genuine as the due diligence has been performed on each and every investor

Are there any Fees associated to FindCapital?

Yes. FindCapital charges 3% on the amounts raised below 20 lacs, 5% on the amounts raised above 20 lacs and below 1 crore and 7% on the amounts raised over 1 crore.
